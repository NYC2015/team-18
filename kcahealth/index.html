Facts about HIV/AIDS

3 out of 4 children living with HIV are still not receiving the treatment they need.

HIV/AIDS is the leading cause of death amongst adolescents, after traffic accidents.

240,000 children were infected in 2013, a new infection every two minutes.

1,000 young women are newly infected every day
